# Design Guidelines for Level Up Pune - Dr. Shrikant Kallurkar

## Design Philosophy
Create a polished, authoritative website inspired by leadcrestconsulting.com's modern UI/UX, adapted to convey deep academic prestige, executive credibility, and 45 years of professional excellence. The design must project wisdom, authority, and world-class institutional trust.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Charcoal Gray**: #212529 (text and dark sections for seriousness and professionalism)
- **Maroon/Burgundy**: #800000 (headlines, buttons, highlights - conveys academic prestige)
- **Pure White**: #FFFFFF (clean backgrounds)
- **Off-White/Light Gray**: #F8F9FA (secondary backgrounds for premium feel and excellent readability)

## Typography
- **Headings**: "Lora" (serif for traditional, academic authority)
- **Body Text**: "Inter" (clean, modern readability)

## Visual Design Elements
- **Glassmorphism**: Semi-transparent blur effects, especially on navbar
- **Soft Shadows**: Professional depth on cards and interactive elements
- **Smooth Hover Effects**: On all interactive elements
- **Generous Whitespace**: Clean, structured grids for sophisticated, uncluttered feel

## Layout & Spacing
- Clean, structured grid system
- Generous whitespace between sections for premium feel
- Single-page smooth-scrolling layout that tells a story of expertise

## Animations
- Subtle, professional on-scroll fade-in animations
- Smooth transitions, never distracting
- Professional hover states on all interactive elements

## Key Sections Design

### Navbar
- Sticky positioning with semi-transparent blur effect
- Logo on left, navigation links center, CTA button ("Book a Consultation") on right

## Responsiveness
Flawless display across all devices is mandatory. Grid layouts should adapt gracefully from desktop to mobile.

## Interactive Elements
- All buttons use maroon/burgundy (#800000) as primary color
- Smooth hover effects with subtle scale or shadow changes
- Form validation with clear error states
- Success messages for form submissions

## Overall Aesthetic
The design must communicate: Authority • Academic Excellence • Executive Credibility • 45 Years of Trust • World-Class Institutional Recognition
